Frequently Asked Questions

What types of yarn do you sell?

We offer a wide variety of yarns including cotton, wool, acrylic, and blended fibers. Each product page has detailed information about the yarn's weight, fiber content, and recommended hook size.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can get a shipping estimate at checkout before placing your order.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns on unused yarn and tools within 30 days of purchase. Please see our Shipping & Returns page for full details on how to initiate a return.

Are your patterns beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! We have a wide range of patterns suitable for all skill levels, from absolute beginners to advanced crocheters. Each pattern clearly indicates its difficulty level. We also have a Tutorials section to help you get started with the basic stitches.
