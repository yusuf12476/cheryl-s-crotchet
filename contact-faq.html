Yes, we love creating custom pieces! Please use the contact form on this page to send us your ideas, including the type of item, desired colors, and any specific patterns you have in mind. We'll get back to you to discuss the details and provide a quote.

Since all items are handmade, please allow 5-7 business days for your order to be crafted. Once shipped, domestic orders typically arrive within 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been dispatched.

To ensure your crochet items last a long time, we recommend hand washing them in cold water with a gentle detergent. Lay flat to dry and avoid wringing or twisting the item. Do not bleach or iron.
